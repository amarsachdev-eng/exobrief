Privacy Policy

Last updated: 13 June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how A Star Supply (trading as EXOBRIEF) ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our service at exobrief.com.

1. Who We Are

EXOBRIEF is operated by A Star Supply, a sole trader based in London, United Kingdom. You can contact us at hello@exobrief.com.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information directly from you:

  • Email address
  • Company name and sector
  • Competitor names you ask us to monitor
  • Strategic priorities and business context you provide

We collect billing information through our payment processor Stripe. We do not store your card details.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Generate and deliver your weekly EXOBRIEF intelligence brief
  • Process your subscription payment
  • Send service-related communications
  • Improve the quality of our intelligence briefs over time

4. Third Party Services

We share your information with the following third party services to deliver the EXOBRIEF service:

  • Stripe — payment processing (Privacy Policy)
  • SendGrid (Twilio) — email delivery (Privacy Policy)
  • Supabase — secure data storage (Privacy Policy)
  • Anthropic — AI brief generation (Privacy Policy)

We do not sell your personal information to any third party.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as you maintain an active subscription with EXOBRIEF. Upon cancellation, we will delete your data within 30 days upon request.

6. Your Rights (UK GDPR)

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Object to processing of your information
  • Data portability

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@exobrief.com.

7. International Data Transfers

Some of our third party service providers (including Anthropic and Supabase) are based in the United States. Data transfers are protected by Standard Contractual Clauses as approved by the European Commission.

8. Cookies

EXOBRIEF does not use cookies or tracking technologies on our website.

9. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify subscribers by email when material changes are made.

11. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at hello@exobrief.com.
